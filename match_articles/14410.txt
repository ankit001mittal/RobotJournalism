Arsenal overcome Everton 3-1 with 10 men.
The Gunners got off to the perfect start when Hector Bellerin gave them the lead after eight minutes.
Alexis Sanchez made it 2-0 on 27 minutes despite the dismissal of Laurent Koscielny; a straight red card
Everton pulled one back through Romelu Lukaku's 25th goal of the season with a penalty awarded. Aaron Ramsey then curled in a superb goal to make it 3-1.
