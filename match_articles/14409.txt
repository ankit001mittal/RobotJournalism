Liverpool won comfortably as Philippe Coutinho scored twice and created another to lead his side to victory at West Ham United.
The visitors started strongly, Coutinho played in Daniel Sturridge who rounded goalkeeper to open the scoring. Liverpool took control in the second half, Coutinho adding a second.
Coutinho fired home a second from a swift break before Divock Origi completed the scoring on 76 minutes with a close-range finish.
