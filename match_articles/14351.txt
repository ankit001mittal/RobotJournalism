Crystal Palace earned a hard fought 3-0 victory over Arsenal at Selhurst Park.
Andros Townsend opened the scoring in the 17th minute, converting from close range after Wilfried Zaha teed him up despite losing his footing.
The impressive Zaha was the creator again, cutting back in similar fashion for Yohan Cabaye to curl a superb effort into the far right corner of Arsenal's goal just after the hour mark.
Five minutes later, Luka Milivojevic finding the left corner with his spot-kick.
