Manchester United were held by 10-man AFC Bournemouth at Old Trafford.
Manchester United dominated from the start and led when Marcos Rojo deflected in Antonio Valencia’s shot on 23 minutes.
AFC Bournemouth were level after 40 minutes when Marc Pugh was brought down and Joshua King converted from the spot against his former team.
The Cherries’ hopes of a result were hit when Andrew Surman received a second yellow card just before half-time, but they hold on to earn the point till the end.
