Liverpool came out on top of Arsenal, beating them 3-1 at Anfield.
The hosts took the lead on nine minutes when Roberto Firmino controlled Sadio Mane’s centre and beat the goalkeeper.
Jurgen Klopp’s side dominated the first half and were two goals up five minutes before the interval when Firmino this time teed up Mane to score.
Arsenal threatened a fightback when half-time substitute Alexis Sanchez's through-ball allowed Danny Welbeck to beat the goalkeeper on 57 minutes,
But Liverpool made sure of the points in stoppage time when Georginio Wijnaldum scored on the break.
