Tottenham Hotspur beats Manchester United 2-1 thanks to goals by Victor Wanyama and Harry Kane. Wanyama got the celebrations started on six minutes, heading in Ben Davies’ centre.
The hosts might have scored more in a dominant first half,later Kane got his goal against Man Utd, in the 48th minute, helping on Christian Eriksen’s free-kick, although Wayne Rooney threatened to poop the party when converting Anthony Martial’s 71st-minute cross.
