Swansea City recorded their victory as they beat West Bromwich Albion 2-1 at the Liberty Stadium.
West Bromwich Albion opened the scoring in a first half full of chances.
Jonny Evans scored the goal, rising highest to head in a corner from Darren Fletcher on 33 minutes.
The hosts drew level on 72 minutes, Jordan Ayew heading home having been found by Fernando Llorente following a set-piece.
Llorente then found the back of the net himself finding the bottom left corner to secure the three points.
