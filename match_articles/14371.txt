AFC Bournemouth recorded win as they beat Middlesbrough 4-0 at Vitality Stadium.
Joshua King continued his excellent form, prodding in the opener after just two minutes, before Benik Afobe beat the offside trap to double the hosts' lead on 16 minutes.
Boro were down to 10 men four minutes later when Gaston Ramirez was shown a second yellow card for a foul on Marc Pugh.
In the second half, Pugh and Charlie Daniels both netted with long-range efforts five minutes apart.
