A Harry Kane hat-trick lifted Tottenham Hotspur as they beat Stoke City 4-0. All the goals came in the first half at White Hart Lane, Kane finishing from an angle and then from outside the box to put Spurs 2-0 up on 32 minutes.
A deflected free-kick gave Kane his hat-trick. He also crossed for Dele Alli to make it 4-0 in first-half stoppage time.
