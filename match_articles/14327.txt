Tottenham Hotspur earned 3 points with a hard fought 2-1 victory over Southampton.The home side took the lead on 14 minutes through Christian Eriksen's fine left-footed finish into the bottom corner.
Dele Alli then doubled Spurs' lead from the penalty spot on 33 minutes after he was fouled.
Saints found a way back into the contest when James Ward-Prowse scored seven minutes into the second period but despite putting on the pressure, they could not claim an equaliser.
