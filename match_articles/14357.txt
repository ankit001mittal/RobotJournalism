Tottenham Hotspur earned a rousing home win over Watford.
Goals by Dele Alli, Eric Dier and a Son Heung-min double earned Mauricio Pochettino’s side home victory.
Alli curled in from range on 33 minutes. Dier doubled the lead from the area’s edge, and Son beat Watford's goalkeeper on 44 minutes. Son converted Kieran Trippier’s cross 10 minutes later.
