Manchester City claimed another 3 points after a 2-0 win at AFC Bournemouth. City took control from the start of the game, shortly after Raheem Sterling converted Leroy Sane’s cross in the 29th minute.
City were 2-0 up after 68 minutes when Sergio Aguero, an early substitute for Gabriel Jesus, poked in Sterling’s cross, although the final touch came from Tyrone Mings.
