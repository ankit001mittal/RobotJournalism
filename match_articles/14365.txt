Stoke City earned a 3-1 victory over Hull City at the bet365 stadium.
The hosts made their early dominance count in the sixth minute, Marko Arnautovic bending the ball into the top corner.
Harry Maguire equalised for the Tigers shortly after half-time, his shot taking a deflection before beating the goalkeeper. Jonathan Walters crossed for Peter Crouch, who scored the goal.
Xherdan Shaqiri settled the match with a fierce shot into the top corner from Arnautovic's pass.
