Manchester City got a 3-1 home victory over Hull City.
Hull goalkeeper Eldin Jakupovic was beaten on 31 minutes by Ahmed Elmohamady's own goal from Jesus Navas's cross.
Marco Silva's side threatened on the counter-attack but fell further behind when Raheem Sterling set up Sergio Aguero for the goal. 
Fabian Delph's thumping strike ensured Man City tightened their grip on the 3 points despite Andrea Ranocchia netting a consolation for Hull late on.
