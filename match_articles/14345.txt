Three late goals by Dele Alli, Son Heung-min and Christian Eriksen gave Tottenham Hotspur a triumphant victory over Swansea City.
The hosts clinched an early lead after Jordan Ayew crossed for Wayne Routledge to angle a low drive past the goalkeeper. Tottenham Hotspur, enjoying most of the possession, finally broke Swansea's resitance in the 88th minute. Alli, latched on to a loose ball to fire into an empty net. Son and Eriksen struck in stoppage time to ensure Tottenham Hotspur victory.
