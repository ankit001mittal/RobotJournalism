Swansea City boosted with a 2-0 win over relegated Sunderland at the Stadium of Light.
The visitors took the lead after nine minutes. Gylfi Sigurdsson's free-kick was nodded in by Fernando Llorente. Just before the break, Swansea doubled their advantage. Ki Sung-yueng found Kyle Naughton and the right-back struck goal with a brilliant effort into the top corner.
Sunderland came close to scoring after the break, but Swansea saw out the match.
