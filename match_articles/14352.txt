Two goals from Everton’s Romelu Lukaku handed Leicester City a defeat, losing 4-2 at Goodison Park.
Everton’s Tom Davies scored within a minute. Islam Slimani levelled three minutes later from a Leicester breakaway.
The visitors were ahead on 10 minutes as Marc Albrighton’s free-kick from wide left went in.
Lukaku’s header from Ross Barkley’s cross levelled the scores again midway through the half and Phil Jagielka put Everton ahead at the break.
Lukaku later finishing in a corner at the far post.
