Cesc Fabregas got a goal and an assist as Chelsea defeated Swansea City 3-1 to add 3 more points in their tally.
Fabregas, gathered Pedro’s pass and prodded home in the 19th minute.
Fernando Llorente levelled for Swansea on the stroke of half-time, heading in with the visitors’ first effort on target.
But a Pedro strike squirmed under the goalkeeper and Eden Hazard teed up Diego Costa as Chelsea struck twice inside the final 20 minutes to secure a dominant home win.
