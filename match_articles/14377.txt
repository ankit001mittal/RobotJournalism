Manchester City completed a 3-1 victory over West Bromwich Albion.
Goals by Gabriel Jesus, Kevin De Bruyne and Yaya Toure ensured an easy win for Pep Guardiola's side.
City started strongly and were ahead on 27 minutes when De Bruyne set up an assist for Jesus.
Two minutes later Jesus turned provider for De Bruyne to bury a first-time shot.
On 57 minutes Yaya Toure exchanged passes with Sergio Aguero and finished with assurance.
Hal Robson-Kanu grabbed an 87th-minute consolation.
