Pedro Obiang scored and assisted as West Ham United won a Premier League match at Southampton. 
January signing Manolo Gabbiadini lashed in off the crossbar to give Saints the lead but Andy Carroll's goal made it 1-1 after 14 minutes. 
Obiang assisted Carroll's goal and the midfielder gave West Ham the lead with a 25-yard effort just before half-time. Mark Noble made it 3-1 seven minutes into the second half with a low free-kick that beat the goalkeeper.
