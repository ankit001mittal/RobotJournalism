Joshua King scored a hat-trick as AFC Bournemouth secured a 3-2 victory over West Ham United at Vitality Stadium.
Soon after the start, West Ham winger teed up Michail Antonio to for the visitors’ opener.
King brilliantly controlled and finished to level on 31 minutes and later gave Bournemouth a 2-1 lead. Andre Ayew converted West Ham’s second from Sam Byram’s cut-back with seven minutes remaining, but King sealed the Cherries’ win when he followed up Jack Wilshere’s saved effort in the 90th minute.
