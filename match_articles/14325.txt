Chelsea extended their points tally by another 3 points as Gary Cahill’s late goal helped them to a 2-1 win at Stoke City.
Willian’s goal on 13 minutes eluded the goalkeeper at the near post to give Chelsea the lead.
Stoke equalised through Jonathan Walters’ penalty after he was fouled.
Chelsea sought to restore their lead and their efforts were rewarded when Cahill reacted quickest to a loose ball in the area after a corner 12 minutes before the end. Phil Bardsley’s red card compounded matters for Stoke.
