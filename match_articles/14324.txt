Manchester United claimed the victory in the Premier League thanks to a hard-fought 3-1 win at Middlesbrough. Middlesbrough, started brightly but Marouane Fellaini put United ahead with a close-range header on the half-hour mark. Jesse Lingard then doubled United's lead on 62 minutes with a superb strike into the top corner from outside the area.
The home side pulled a goal back through Rudy Gestede with 13 minutes left but despite piling on the pressure, United sealed the three points when Antonio Valencia tapped in the ball into the post.
