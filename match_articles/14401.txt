Goals from Wilfried Zaha, Christian Benteke, Luka Milivojevic and Patrick van Aanholt gave Crystal Palace a 4-0 victory over Hull City. Palace had the perfect start, Zaha pouncing on mistakes in the Hull City defence to score after only three minutes.
The Eagles doubled their lead on 34 minutes when Benteke rose highest to head home from Jason Puncheon's corner. Milivojevic made it 3-0 with an 85th-minute penalty while Van Aanholt completed the scoring in the final minute.
