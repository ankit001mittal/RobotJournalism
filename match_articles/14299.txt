West Bromwich Albion claimed 3 points with a 2-1 victory over AFC Bournemouth at The Hawthorns.
Bournemouth took the lead inside five minutes when Joshua King converted from the penalty spot.
The home side hit back quickly as Craig Dawson’s deflected long-range effort and Gareth McAuley’s tap-in from a corner put West Bromwich in charge at the break.
