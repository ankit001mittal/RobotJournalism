Romelu Lukaku's goal helped Everton in claiming 3-0 win over West Bromwich Albion.
Kevin Mirallas broke the deadlock in the 39th minute, pouncing after Baggies goalkeeper had saved Ross Barkley’s shot. Lukaku slipped the ball through for Morgan Schneiderlin to double the lead just before the interval.
The Everton striker added gloss to the scoreline in the 82nd minute, heading home Barkley’s cross.
