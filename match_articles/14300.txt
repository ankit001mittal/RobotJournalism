Leicester City fought back from a goal down against Hull City to win. Leicester fell behind to Sam Clucas's goal on 14 minutes, assisted by Kamil Grosicki.
Christian Fuchs levelled before half-time after good work with Jamie Vardy and Riyad Mahrez completed the turnaround on the hour with his goal from the edge of the box.
Tom Huddlestone's late own goal sealed the win to lift Leicester.
